## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**Passes AI slop test.** Restrained palette, dark-first, mono-for-data typography, functional animations. Violations: nested cards in findings, inconsistent component vocabulary across admin.

**Technical scan** (67 findings): 6 hardcoded colors (SVG snapshot), 13 a11y (missing alt, unassociated labels, opacity contrast risk), 17 consistency (border-radius, focus rings, metric grids), 4 CSS/animation, 27 duplicated code.

## Overall Impression

Solid visual foundation with clear domain identity. Weak point is organizational: duplicated admin code and nested card hierarchy are growth symptoms. Individual screens work; the product as a cohesive system does not.

## What's Working

1. Domain-anchored vocabulary (4/4 heuristic)
2. Smart parsing of unstructured results into 5 consumption views
3. Follow-up as mode shift, not toggle
4. Animation discipline (ease-out, prefers-reduced-motion, no layout animations)
